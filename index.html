01 — The idea

We didn't build a hotel. We built a place where your nervous system can finally exhale.

Modern life leaves you wired and depleted at the same time. Recalibrate is a sanctuary engineered to reverse that — combining the science of recovery with the slow rhythm of the wilderness.

Your phone goes in a wooden box at check-in. What replaces it: a curated rhythm of movement, contrast, stillness, and human connection that lasts longer than any notification ever did.

02 — Restorative technology

Science you can feel
in your body.

Every protocol is built around evidence-based recovery, then personalized by an AI that learns your physiology over the course of your stay.

Thermal

Contrast therapy

Cedar sauna at 90°C. Glacial plunge at 4°C. Repeat. A 3000-year-old practice, refined into the most effective reset for inflammation and mood you'll find.

Adaptive

AI-personalized recovery

Wearables read your HRV, sleep, and stress load. Your day adapts in real time — more breathwork on hard mornings, more movement on the easy ones.

Aquatic

Hydrotherapy circuits

Mineral pools, sensory float tanks, Kneipp walks. Water as medicine — guided by therapists who measure outcomes, not just minutes.

A phone-free promise

03 — Social by design

Wellness shouldn't
feel like solitary confinement.

We borrowed the best parts of summer camp, film festivals, and dinner parties — and rebuilt them around presence. You'll leave with new friends, not just a lower cortisol score.

Weekly

Open-air
cinema nights

Curated indie films projected onto a 30-foot screen under the stars. Wool blankets, hot toddies, and a quiet rule: no talking during the credits.

Daily

Photobooth
hunts

Twelve hidden polaroid stations across the valley. Find them all, take them home.

Workshops

Make
something
